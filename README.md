SKYWARD FLAPPY BALANCE - USER MANUAL
DOSAGE: Play anytime for 5-30 minutes of fun!

HOW TO PLAY:
• Click START or press SPACE to begin your journey
• Press SPACE or tap the screen to make your bird fly upward
• Guide your bird carefully through the pipe gaps to score points
• Avoid hitting pipes or the ground - one touch and game over!
• Need a break? Press ESC or click the ⏸️ button to pause anytime

SCORING SYSTEM:
• Each pipe you successfully pass = 1 point
• Every 20 points triggers a magical theme change!
• Your high score is automatically saved in your browser

THEME CHANGES:
The game transforms every 20 points! Watch the sky transition from beautiful daylight to mysterious night mode. The background, colors, and atmosphere completely change - keeping each gaming session fresh and exciting. Day theme features bright blue skies and golden sunlight, while night theme brings dark blue hues and a peaceful moonlit ambiance.

SIDE EFFECTS:
Highly addictive gameplay! Warning: May cause competitive urges, frequent "just one more game" syndrome, and extreme happiness when beating your high score. Not responsible for lost sleep or neglected chores!

STORAGE:
Your best scores are safely stored in your browser's local storage. They'll be waiting for you even after you close and reopen the game!

TIPS FOR BEGINNERS:
• Start with gentle taps rather than hard presses
• Focus on the gap, not your bird
• Practice makes perfect - don't get discouraged!
• Take breaks between games to reset your focus

TROUBLESHOOTING:
• Game not responding? Refresh the page
• Custom bird not showing? Make sure it's a PNG file under 5MB
• Scores resetting? Check if your browser allows local storage

REMEMBER: The key to success is rhythm and patience. Keep flapping and soon you'll be soaring through those pipes like a pro!
